Lead doctor

Mark B Gano II DMD, MSD

Providing personalized endodontic care with a focus on comfort, education, and long-term patient trust.

Dr. Gano embraces an ongoing commitment to a culture of excellence and to serving his community. As a Diplomate of the American Board of Endodontics, Dr. Gano became East Louisville's only Board-Certified Endodontist and one of only two practicing endodontists in Louisville. The Board Certification process, which takes years to accomplish, is only given to those who promote the highest quality of care for their patients. Those selected and awarded with this distinction demonstrate superior diagnostic skill, excellent clinical proficiency, and outstanding professional judgment. Less than a quarter of endodontists reach this level of certification. Because of this honor, Dr. Gano is excited to share his expertise by providing top-quality endodontics to the Louisville area. To do that, he utilizes the latest technology to help his patients receive the best care. As Louisville's first and only provider to incorporate the GentleWave® procedure, he leads the market as he highlights his ongoing commitment to the most advanced endodontic care possible.
