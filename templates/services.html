Diagnosis

Proper diagnosis is the most important part of your treatment. Oral pain such as toothaches or cracked/fractured teeth can often be difficult to pinpoint. Because of the vast network of nerves in the mouth, the pain of a damaged or diseased tooth is often felt in another tooth and/or in the head, neck or ear. An endodontist is a specialist in diagnosing and treating this type of pain.
